What is BankSignal?

BankSignal is a SaaS platform that brings together regulatory filings, financial performance data, and curated industry news into a single, focused view for banking professionals. It tracks every FDIC-insured bank and NCUA-chartered credit union in the United States.

Rather than assembling spreadsheets from multiple government sources, BankSignal normalizes quarterly Call Report data and computes the metrics that matter — efficiency ratios, asset quality indicators, capital adequacy, deposit and loan growth — bringing performance into focus.

Capabilities

Institution Explorer

Browse and compare 5,000+ financial institutions with sortable metrics, holding company grouping, and custom tagging.

Financial Intelligence

24-metric audit reports with full calculation transparency. TTM income, growth rates, asset quality, and capital ratios.

Daily Briefing

Curated headlines from regulators, trade publications, and regional sources — filtered to banking and fintech relevance.

Registry Monitoring

Automated detection of new charters, name changes, mergers, and deactivations across both FDIC and NCUA registries.

Publicly Traded Mapping

SEC-sourced stock exchange and ticker data linked to FDIC institutions, with direct connections to market data.

Personal Watchlist

Track the institutions that matter to you. Per-user, private, and fully integrated with all Explorer capabilities.

Data sources

BankSignal ingests and normalizes data from authoritative regulatory sources on a quarterly cycle:

FDIC Call Reports NCUA 5300 Reports SEC EDGAR FFIEC NIC OCC CFPB Federal Reserve

Built for banking professionals

BankSignal is designed for executives and advisors who work with U.S. depository institutions. Whether you are benchmarking peer performance, monitoring a competitive landscape, or staying current on regulatory developments, the platform provides a structured, no-noise alternative to manual research.

How it was built

BankSignal started as a personal project to test what is possible when a non-developer partners with Claude, Anthropic's AI assistant. Every aspect of the platform — from regulatory data research and database schema design to the full-stack application, Azure cloud infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and automated GitHub Actions workflows — was designed, planned, and built through conversation with Claude Code.

No templates. No boilerplate. Just iterative collaboration between a domain expert and an AI that can reason about architecture, write production code, and debug deployment issues in real time.
